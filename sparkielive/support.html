Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I get an API key?

SparkieLive requires an API key to reach its backend. Keys are issued directly — email us at the address below to request access.

"No connection" or slow answers

Answers are generated by a live model, so complex questions can take up to a minute. If you see a connection error: check your signal or Wi-Fi, wait a moment, and tap Retry. If it persists, the server may be under load — try again shortly.

Is my chat history private?

Yes. Chat history and your API key are stored only on your device. There are no accounts and no tracking. See the Privacy Policy.

Are the answers code-legal?

SparkieLive is a reference aid. Always verify against the published NEC as adopted in your jurisdiction, and confirm with your AHJ before you wire. The app tells you the relevant code sections so you can check fast.

How do I change or remove my API key?

Tap the gear icon in the app to open Settings, where you can update or clear the stored key.
