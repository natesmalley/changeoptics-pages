Dopamenu is a dopamine-menu habit app: build a menu of healthy rewards, track wins, and journal how it felt — all stored privately on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything (menus, journal, voice memos, streaks) lives on your device. There is no account and nothing is uploaded. Deleting the app deletes its data, so back up your device if you want to keep your history.

My voice memo isn't transcribing

Transcription runs on-device via Apple's Speech framework. Check Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition and make sure Dopamenu has microphone permission. On-device dictation may need a one-time language download (Settings → General → Keyboard → Dictation).

How do I restore my Dopamenu Plus purchase?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so use the same Apple ID you bought with. Billing itself is managed by Apple in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Reminders aren't firing

Reminders are local notifications. Check iOS Settings → Notifications → Dopamenu and make sure notifications are allowed, and that Focus modes aren't silencing them.
