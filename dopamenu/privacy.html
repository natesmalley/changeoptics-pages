Privacy Policy for Dopamenu

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Summary

Dopamenu is a local-first app. Your menus, journal entries, mood notes, and voice memos are stored on your device and are never uploaded to us. There is no account and no sign-in. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal content.

Information Stored on Your Device

The following stays on your phone (in on-device storage) and is not transmitted to us:

  • Your menu items and daily selections
  • Journal entries and mood notes
  • Voice memos and their transcriptions
  • App preferences, themes, streaks, and reminder settings

You can delete all of this at any time from within the app.

Voice Memos and Transcription

Voice memos are recorded on your device. Transcription is performed entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework (on-device recognition). Your audio and its transcription never leave your phone and are never sent to us or any third party.

Purchases (Dopamenu Plus)

Dopamenu offers an optional subscription and lifetime purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, our subscription infrastructure provider. To manage your subscription, RevenueCat processes purchase information (such as your subscription status and Apple-provided transaction identifiers). This is used solely to deliver and restore the features you paid for. See RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy. We do not receive your payment card details; Apple handles billing.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device using local notifications. No reminder content is sent to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account credentials (there is no account).
  • We do not collect your journal, mood, menu, or voice data.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or third-party tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information.

Data Storage and Security

Your content is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's standard app data protections. Because it lives on-device, its security is tied to your device passcode/biometrics and any device backups you choose to make (e.g., iCloud/iTunes backups, which are governed by Apple's policies).

Children's Privacy

Dopamenu is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new policy on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.
